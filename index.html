設計理念 / Design Concept

現代人生活忙碌，不一定配合得上垃圾車的時間，
對於老人及行動不便者，倒垃圾是一件相對困難的事。
我們的目標是透過這個平台，讓大家享有更方便的生活。

Busy modern lives can make it difficult to match garbage truck schedules.
For the elderly and those with limited mobility, waste disposal can be challenging.
We aim to create a platform that offers everyone a more convenient lifestyle.
