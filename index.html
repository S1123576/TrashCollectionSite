設計理念 / Our Philosophy

現代人的生活忙碌，未必能配合垃圾車的固定時間；而對於老人及行動不便者，倒垃圾更是極具挑戰。
Modern life is busy, and not everyone can match the fixed schedule of garbage trucks. For the elderly and those with limited mobility, taking out trash can be even more challenging.

我們希望透過這個平台，提供更便捷的垃圾清運服務，讓每個人都能輕鬆維持清潔的生活環境，提升生活品質。
Through this platform, we aim to provide a more convenient waste removal service, enabling everyone to easily maintain a clean living environment and improve their quality of life.
