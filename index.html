設計理念 / Design Concept

現代人生活忙碌，不一定配合得上垃圾車的時間，
以及老人及行動不便者倒垃圾對他們來說相較不易。
我們希望透過這個平台，讓大家有更方便的生活。
Modern life is busy, and not everyone can match garbage truck schedules.
For the elderly and those with mobility challenges, taking out the trash can be difficult.
We aim to make life more convenient through our platform.
